Optimization Techniques

Optimization is the process of finding the best solution from a set of feasible options. It involves minimizing or maximizing an objective function subject to constraints. Optimization is central to operations research, machine learning, control systems, and economics.

1. Core Concepts

  • Objective Function: Function to be optimized (minimized or maximized)
  • Constraints: Conditions that restrict the solution space
  • Feasible Region: Set of all points satisfying constraints
  • Global vs. Local Optimum: Best overall vs. best in a neighborhood

2. Types of Optimization

  • Linear Programming (LP): Linear objective and constraints
  • Integer Programming: Variables restricted to integers
  • Nonlinear Programming: Nonlinear objective or constraints
  • Convex Optimization: Convex objective and feasible region
  • Combinatorial Optimization: Discrete and finite solution spaces
